Splash Screen Approaches

How should the logo splash work and transition to content?

A

Fade Out Splash

Logo fills the screen (2-3 sec), then fades out. Reveals the page with scroll animations. No navigation visible on splash.

Pros

  • Clean, dramatic entrance
  • Clear loading phase
  • Easy to understand

Cons

  • Forces users to wait
  • Less interactive feel
  • Harder on mobile (time feels long)
B

Scroll Reveal

Logo stays at top of page. User scrolls to reveal content beneath. Navigation hidden until scroll. More interactive.

Pros

  • No waiting required
  • Interactive & engaging
  • Guides user to scroll
  • Works well on mobile

Cons

  • Logo might feel "stuck"
  • Less of a splash moment
  • Need clear scroll hint
C

Hero Transition

Logo is large hero section at top. As you scroll, logo scales down & moves to nav. Content animates in. Smooth integration.

Pros

  • Sophisticated feel
  • Logo becomes nav
  • No "loading" wait
  • Modern web pattern

Cons

  • More complex to code
  • Mobile can feel cramped
  • Subtle effect (less dramatic)
